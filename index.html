About shy

shy is an anonymous social app where users share thoughts and feelings without public profile identities. Our goal is to provide a lightweight, safe, and respectful space for self-expression.

What we focus on

  • Anonymous participation by default
  • Safety-first moderation and reporting
  • Fast, low-bandwidth mobile experience
